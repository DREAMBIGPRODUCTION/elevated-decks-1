## Notes on Development

This website is designed to support the following business goals:
1. **Lead Generation** - Capture deck project inquiries via contact forms
2. **Social Proof** - Showcase past projects and testimonials from satisfied clients
3. **Trust Building** - Demonstrate structural expertise and material knowledge
4. **Local SEO** - Target South Florida deck construction searches
5. **Premium Positioning** - Position as high-end custom deck contractor

### Service Areas
Primary focus on South Florida:
- Miami-Dade County: Miami, Coral Gables, Doral, Homestead, etc.
- Broward County: Fort Lauderdale, Hollywood, Pembroke Pines, etc.
- Palm Beach County: West Palm Beach, Boca Raton, etc.

### Design Philosophy
- Industrial, premium aesthetic
- Dark theme (charcoal/black backgrounds)
- Metallic accents and modern typography
- Focus on structural elements and engineering
- High-quality imagery of deck projects
